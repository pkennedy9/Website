America's Pastime Reviews

Hello from Jon and Peter! This website was created in the hopes of consolidating all information about the MLB stadiums, both in the American and National League. Information about any stadium, including their events and ticket information can be found on any individual stadium's page.

Here, fans can rate their experiences at various stadiums to help other fans decide whether or not they want to attend an event happening at a certain stadium. To see fan's reviews or leave your own review, please sign up using the link at the top right of the website.

How to Navigate

At the top of the page you are able to browse MLB ballparks depedning on which League they are in, whether that be the National League or the American League. When you click on an individual stadium, you will be directed to a new page with all of the information for that stadium.

You will also see a Rankings tab at the top. After a long review of each stadium, we put together a rankings page based on a number of factors. Some examples include the food, beer selection, stadium atmosphere, and more. Feel free to check out the our rankings, and if you have any different thoughts, write a comment in our reviews page and let us know your rankings!

To learn more about the creater's of America's Pastime Reviews, click on the About tab up top. There you will find information about us and attached will be our contact information if you would like to get in touch to talk about baseball or other sports.

On the left we have each MLB team listed with more information about the team and their stadium. Click on your favorite team to check out their page and leave a comment about your experience!

Keep scrolling down for more content! Enjoy!

Stadium News

Busch Stadium

The St. Louis Cardinals recently renovated their Party Suites as part of a multi-million dollar project aimed at enhancing the experience for groups. A total of 21 Party Suites have be given an updated look. They have been updated to host groups of 32 or more and now feature a buffet and full-service bar.

Fenway Park

The Boston Red Sox are on track to add another round of renovations to MLB's oldest stadium. The updates include bleacher upgrades, a new function room, and a new 5,000-seat theater in collaboration with Live Nation is planned at Lansdowne and Ipswich streets. They plan to finish these renovations before the start of the 2021 opening.

Dodger Stadium

The Los Angeles Dodgers updated their stadium prior to the 2020 season to include a new center field plaza that features food and beverage areas, children's play area, and a space for live entertainment. The renovations also include a new outfield entryway, where the legendary Jackie Robinson statue will be on display, and much more.
